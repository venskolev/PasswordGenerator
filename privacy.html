Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

At SecurePass, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to improve our service:

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., pages visited, time spent). This data is anonymized.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to store your preferences (such as your consent to this policy) for up to 1 month.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Analyzing website traffic and performance.
  • Improving the user experience and functionality of SecurePass.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how our website is used. Google Analytics may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system. Google's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to disable cookies through your browser settings. You can also opt-out of Google Analytics tracking.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@secure-pass.app.
